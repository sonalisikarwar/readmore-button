Corona viruses



​​

Coronaviruses are a group of related RNA viruses that cause diseases in mammals and birds. In humans,
these viruses cause respiratory tract infections that can range from mild to lethal.
Mild illnesses include some cases of the common cold (which is also caused by other viruses, predominantly rhinoviruses),
while more lethal varieties can cause SARS, MERS, and COVID-19. Symptoms in other species
vary: in chickens, they cause an upper respiratory tract disease, while in cows and pigs they
cause diarrhea. There are as yet no v...accines or antiviral drugs to prevent or treat human coronavirus infections.
